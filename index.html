About

I'm a product data analyst who owns analytics end to end — from defining what gets measured (event tracking and metric definitions) to modeling data in SQL and Snowflake, to building the Tableau, Power BI, and Adobe Analytics dashboards teams actually use.

My focus is identity and authentication analytics: registration and login funnels, conversion, adoption, and product health. I set the KPI definitions and governance that product and leadership treat as the single source of truth, and I root-cause the drop-offs so engineering can fix them.
